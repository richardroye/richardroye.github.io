About

Keith Roye is the Founder & Chief Operations Officer of Plato Alpha Design a custom software development company whose mission and purpose is to do its part in revolutionizing/pioneering the digital age within the country of The Bahamas by any means necessary.

He is a firm believer that having a growth mindset will lead to measurable results. Keith is currently building the next generation of digital solutions for The Bahamas.

Keith has worked as the lead developer on various projects for companies such as Doctors Hospital, Cable Bahamas, Aliv, Rotary Clubs and the Bahamian Government.
